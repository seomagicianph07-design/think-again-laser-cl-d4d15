Frequently Asked Questions About Hyperpigmentation and Hypopigmentation Correction

How much does hyperpigmentation or hypopigmentation correction cost at Think Again Laser Clinic in Brisbane?
The cost for pigment correction at Think Again Laser Clinic varies depending on the size of the area and the degree of pigmentation. Most treatments start from $150 per session for small areas. During your initial consultation at our Brisbane clinic, we’ll assess your skin and provide a clear price estimate tailored to your needs.
How many sessions will I need to correct hyperpigmentation or hypopigmentation?
The number of sessions depends on factors like pigment depth, your skin type, and how your skin responds to treatment. Most clients in Brisbane see noticeable improvement after 2–4 sessions, spaced 4–6 weeks apart. Some may require additional treatments for optimal results. We’ll develop a personalised treatment plan during your first visit.
Is your staff licensed and experienced in treating pigmentation issues?
Absolutely. All our clinicians at Think Again Laser Clinic Brisbane are fully qualified and certified in laser and pigmentation correction procedures. We only use TGA-approved medical-grade lasers, and our team undergoes regular training to ensure the highest safety and efficacy standards.
What does the pigment correction process involve, and is there any downtime?
Our process starts with a thorough consultation and patch test to determine the best approach for your skin. Treatments use advanced lasers specifically calibrated for pigmentation correction. Most sessions take 15–30 minutes. You may experience mild redness or swelling afterwards, but most clients can resume normal activities immediately. We provide detailed aftercare instructions to protect your skin in the Brisbane climate.
Do you guarantee results for pigmentation correction treatments?
While we use industry-leading technology and customise each treatment, individual results can vary due to factors like skin type and pigment depth. We’re committed to honest advice and realistic expectations, and we track your progress at every visit. If you have concerns, we’ll happily discuss alternative approaches or additional sessions to help you achieve the best possible outcome.
