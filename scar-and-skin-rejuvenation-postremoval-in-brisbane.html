How many sessions will I need to remove my tattoo, and how long does the process take?
The number of sessions required for complete tattoo removal varies based on factors such as ink colours, tattoo depth, skin type, and your body’s natural healing response. On average, most clients in Brisbane require between 6 and 10 sessions, spaced 6-8 weeks apart. Lighter colours and older tattoos may take fewer treatments, while vibrant or layered tattoos can take longer. We’ll discuss a realistic timeline and expected outcomes during your initial consultation.
